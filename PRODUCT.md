# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, interviewers, and operations or marketing team leads review this site while screening or preparing to interview Wang Kejie for content, new media, market, brand, or product operations internships.

## Product Purpose

This is a PC-first personal portfolio website that turns resume evidence into a visual, browsable case experience. It should quickly communicate a candidate who combines content operations, visual production, commercial shooting, campus execution, and AI/data workflow ability.

## Brand Personality

Restrained, visual, and precise. The experience should feel premium and confident without becoming a generic portfolio template or a decorative resume.

## Anti-references

Avoid playful neon styling, template-like card grids, a pure photographer portfolio direction, a flat electronic resume, excessive decorative gradients, and motion that distracts from reading.

## Design Principles

- Show real evidence before claims.
- Keep the dark visual system calm, readable, and image-led.
- Let Works combine projects, experiences, and capability proof instead of acting as a simple image wall.
- Preserve a PC-first presentation with stable layout and no text overlap.
- Keep source assets intact and use project-local web assets for preview-specific crops.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Prioritize readable contrast on the dark background, visible hover and focus states, closable dialogs, reduced-motion support, and no hidden phone-number exposure.
